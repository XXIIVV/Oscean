    Rewriting Playground

    Drag symbols from the creation pane to create things. Step through the evaluation by pressing the button with the squiggly symbol to rewrite the symbols in the bag, or press the arrow button to apply the rules backward.

    • Creation Pane, left
    • Rules List, middle
    • The Bag, right

    Manual

    Click on the symbols in the creation pane to open the Symbol Editor, when in this mode, you can draw new symbols, save/load the ICN graphics, lock/unlock the dragging of that symbol, activate continuous/step mode for when the symbol is clicked on. Clicking inside the bag during symbol editing gives that symbol an anchor position, when the symbol will created as the result of a rule, it will be spawned at that location.

    You can download the playground and use it locally with a Varvara emulator, or build it from the source.
